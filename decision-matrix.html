Recommendation & Rationale

Strengths
Highest score on Vendor Maturity (9/10) — strongest local support network and APRA engagement record of the three options. Technical Fit score of 8/10 confirms alignment with target-state architecture. Implementation risk rated manageable at 7/10, with an existing delivery team in-market.
Conditions
Approval is conditional on: (1) reference check with two comparable APRA-regulated institutions completed within 30 days; (2) fixed-price delivery commitment from vendor covering Phase 1 and Phase 2; (3) independent review of TCO assumptions by Group Finance prior to contract execution.
Risks
TCO is the weakest dimension at 6/10 — licensing and integration costs are above benchmark. If cost assumptions are not locked before contract, overrun risk is material. UX score of 7/10 warrants a targeted change management programme ahead of rollout to minimise adoption friction.
Fallback
If reference checks reveal material concerns or vendor cannot commit to fixed-price terms, Crestline Suite (score 68.4) is the recommended fallback. Its TCO advantage and strong regulatory compliance posture (9/10) make it viable under a cost-constrained scenario, subject to a separate technical fit assessment.
