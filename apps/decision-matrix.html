Recommendation & Rationale

Strengths
Highest score on Provider Maturity (9/10), with the strongest documentation, support model, and implementation playbook of the three options. Workflow Fit score of 8/10 confirms alignment with the target operating model. Implementation risk is rated manageable at 7/10, supported by a clear onboarding path and reusable configuration patterns.
Conditions
Approval is conditional on: (1) completion of two comparable reference checks within 30 days; (2) confirmed delivery plan covering setup, configuration, and onboarding; (3) independent review of maintenance effort, support requirements, and internal ownership before rollout.
Risks
Ongoing Effort is the weakest dimension at 6/10, with configuration, training, and administration requirements above the preferred benchmark. If ownership is not clarified before rollout, adoption may drift. UX score of 7/10 also warrants a targeted change-management plan.
Fallback
If reference checks reveal material concerns or the delivery plan cannot be confirmed, Crestline Suite is the recommended fallback. Its lower ongoing effort and strong governance posture make it viable under a simpler operating model, subject to a separate workflow-fit assessment.
