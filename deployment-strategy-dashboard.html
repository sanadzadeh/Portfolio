Programme Context

This fictional programme migrates selected correspondent banking settlement flows from legacy Vostro/Nostro account structures toward blockchain-enabled settlement and regulated stablecoin liquidity rails. The objective is not to replace all SWIFT activity immediately, but to prove whether high-friction corridors can be settled faster, reconciled more cleanly, and governed within existing prudential, AML/CTF, and liquidity-control expectations.
