Programme Context

This fictional programme supports a multinational wealth management fund moving selected fund-subscription, redemption, treasury, and inter-entity settlement flows from batch-based legacy processes toward blockchain-enabled settlement. The objective is not to tokenise the entire operating model at once. The first stage proves whether high-friction cross-border settlement can be completed faster, reconciled automatically, governed through permissioned controls, and integrated with custody, transfer-agency, fund-accounting, and liquidity-management processes.

Executive Attention
Custody and key control: private-key governance, signer authority, and recovery procedures remain the main go-live constraints.
Operating-model risk: legacy settlement and blockchain settlement must run in parallel until reconciliation confidence improves.
Technology path: permissioned settlement rail and tokenised cash workflow are progressing, subject to audit and legal finalisation.
